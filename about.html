Hi, Iker here.

I'm a recently graduated master's student in Strategic Design of Product and Services. I consider myself a versatile designer, whose greatest virtue is to be able to adapt to any stage, task and design problem.

In my first professional years, I have worked both as a UX Designer and as a Strategic Designer. As a UX Designer, I was responsible for the design of a web platform that helps companies in Gipuzkoa to improve their competitiveness in the circular economy. Here, I was involved in the whole product design process, from the earliest stage of research to prototyping and subsequent collaboration with code developers.

As a Strategic Designer at Copreci, I was responsible for designing the roadmap for the induction product portfolio. Thanks to the strategic value of design, we not only managed to design a strategy that would fit the future demands of the market and consumers, but we also trained the company's internal team in people-centered innovation techniques, planting the first seed for the change of the company's culture towards a more consumer-centered way of working and thinking.

Currently, I am working as a product designer at Protecnológica. I have been responsible for the early design and development of products for the agricultural industry. I also have been designing the new website of the company, from the early stage to the interaction.
